# Ads Not Serving Quick Check (Vicky)

If you see no ads/impressions, check these in this order:

1. Campaign status
- Must be Enabled (not Paused).
- Use campaign_upload_enabled.xlsx if needed.

2. Correct campaign selected
- Confirm you are inside Bangalore_Search_Leads_Vicky.
- Your screenshot currently shows another campaign (Pigeon pin codes / Ad group 1).

3. Ad group status
- All imported ad groups must be Enabled.

4. Ad status and approval
- Go to Ads tab and check Status column.
- If Under review/disapproved, wait or fix policy issues.

5. Keywords present and eligible
- Check Keywords tab for active keywords.
- If all are low search volume or limited, expand with more phrase terms.

6. Date range
- Set date to Last 30 days or Today.
- Very narrow date ranges can show zero by mistake.

7. Billing/payment
- Ensure account has active billing and no payment hold.

8. Bid strategy learning
- New campaigns can show low serving for initial hours.
- Keep enabled for 24 hours before judging.

9. Location targeting
- Keep Bengaluru city targeting broad initially.
- Avoid too strict radius during first day.

10. Final URL and site accessibility
- Final URL should load: https://vickysafetynets.co.in/

## Recommended immediate action
1. Upload campaign_upload_enabled.xlsx
2. Confirm you are viewing Bangalore_Search_Leads_Vicky
3. Upload adgroup, keyword, negative, and rsa files again if needed
4. Wait 2-6 hours and check ad/keyword statuses
